cjxl_main.cc (official libjxl tools/cjxl_main.cc)

This is the most complete real-world usage of the entire libjxl encoder option set.
It shows how effort, distance, modular family, progressive, photon_noise, brotli_effort, extra channel handling, etc. are all wired together in a production CLI.

Full raw file: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/libjxl/libjxl/main/tools/cjxl_main.cc

Key areas for reference:
- AddCommandLineOptions() — option registration
- ProcessFlags() — mapping CLI flags to JXLCompressParams / frame settings
- SetDistanceFromFlags()
- Handling of --brotli_effort, --photon_noise_iso, --modular_*, --progressive_*, etc.

Highly recommended to read when implementing any advanced encoder feature.
